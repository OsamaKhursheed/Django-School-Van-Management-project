We prioritize user privacy by displaying only necessary and relevant information drivers to Students and students to Drivers. Your experience is tailored to provide essential details, ensuring the utmost confidentiality and ease of use within our platform.

Our system is fully automated, streamlining various tasks to enhance your experience. From sending essential emails for updates and notifications to generating fee vouchers effortlessly, our platform efficiently assigns drivers to students, ensuring a seamless and hassle-free process.

At School Van Management System, we prioritize open communication and problem resolution. Students can easily voice their concerns about drivers or other issues, while drivers can request fee adjustments or report delays due to unforeseen circumstances. Our system facilitates prompt action by van owners, ensuring a responsive and supportive environment for all

At School Van Management System, user privacy and security are paramount. Our platform ensures that users can access necessary information securely. Students can also voice their concerns anonymously when filing complaints about drivers, guaranteeing a safe and confidential environment for all.

Our system provides notifications for user requests, delivering updates both on the portal and through email. This ensures that users stay informed about their requests, making communication efficient and accessible.

Our system excels in fee management. It automates fee record creation, sending vouchers via email to students. Automated reminders notify students of fee dues. Furthermore, both drivers and students can access their general ledger and fee records, ensuring transparency and convenience

This is my school van management project that is constructed in the Django framework. The project aims to streamline the management of school transportation, providing students, drivers, and owners with a seamless experience. It includes features such as student registration (subject to owner approval), driver registration (also subject to owner approval), request handling for delays and fee increases, viewing driver and student information, and generating fee vouchers.

The system is built on top of my prior experience, where I created similar applications using Tkinter in my 3rd and 4th semesters.

Student

  • Log in
  • Register (subject to owner approval)
  • Update Information
  • View Driver
  • Complain about Driver to Owner
  • View Driver Delay Notifications (subject to owner approval)
  • View Fee Details

Driver

  • Log in
  • Register (subject to owner approval)
  • Request Delay to Van Owner
  • Request Fee Increase
  • View Fee Details (subject to owner approval)
  • View Student Information (subject to owner approval)

Owner

  • Log in
  • Approve or Cancel Requests
  • Generate Fee Voucher
  • Send Email to Parents for Different Purposes
